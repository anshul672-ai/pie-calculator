How it works
FIRE

The number that sets you free

Your retirement corpus. The amount where your investments generate enough to live on forever, without working.

SIP

Wealth, built monthly

A fixed amount invested every month in mutual funds. Small, consistent, compounding — the engine that builds your FIRE corpus.

NPS

Your pension, backed by the govt.

A pension scheme that locks in contributions until 60, gives you a tax-free lump sum plus a monthly pension for life.

EPS

Your salary-linked pension

A slice of your employer's EPF contribution builds a government pension for life. Small monthly, but guaranteed.
